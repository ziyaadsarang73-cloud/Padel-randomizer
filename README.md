# Padel Team Randomizer and Score Keeper

The **Padel Team Randomizer and Score Keeper** app is designed to enhance your Padel experience by randomly creating teams and keeping track of scores during matches. Whether you're playing casually or in a more organized setting, this app aims to streamline the process of team selection and score management.

## Features:
- **Team Randomization:** Easily create balanced teams based on player's skill level.
- **Score Tracking:** Keep track of scores with an intuitive interface.
- **Match History:** View past games and scores for better future matchups.

## Getting Started:
To use the app, simply follow these steps:
1. Launch the app.
2. Input player names.
3. Click 'Randomize Teams' to generate teams.
4. Start playing and keep track of your scores in real-time!
